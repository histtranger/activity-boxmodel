Deadmau5

An electronic music producer who has made a large impact in the music scene.

Joel Zimmerman, or better known as, Deadmau5 to his fans, is his own producer, manager, and owner of Mau5trap records. He started making music in 2005 and since has grown into one of the most knownk names of electronic music beside Calvin Harris and Tiesto.
